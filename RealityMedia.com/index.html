About Us

Reality Media Limited is comprised of a young versatile team of media and sports professionals who have served in the international mainstream television industry.The company started as a partnership in the year 2003 and was incorporated in the year 2006 as a Limited Liability Company in Kenya.

Our Objective

Objectives

Our main objective is to create African content for broadcast, online, corporate and non-governmental clients. We pride ourselves as a truly pan-African digital media house delivering cutting edge creative content and major sporting events.

Our mission

Mission

We are on a mission to inform, entertain, inspire and connect Africa.

Our Vision

Vision

We are inspiring to create a vibrant sport, broadcast, film and media industry in Africa by producing cutting-edge high quality content.

Services

We have over 14 years of experience in Television and Photograph offering cutting-edge production and post-production facilities.
We ensure projects are delivered with both highest quality and efficiency.

Sports Production

We offer live multi-camera production of high profile sports events using our state of the art outside broadcasting technology.

Sports management & Consultancy

We offer sports management and consultancy services especially in Rugby. Our primary project portfolio include media, event management, sport academies, sport tours, sports equipment and construction of sport facilities.

TV Production

We have fully equipped High Defination(HD) outside broadcasting (OB) facility. We produce quality documentary films, feature stories, news report, humanitarian related stories and general entertainment TV production.

Documentaries & Informacials

We research, produce, direct, film and edit commissioned documentaries and help our clients achieve brand ambitions.

Media Campaigns

We organize strategic media campaigns to promote various campaigns. We pulled off the most glamorous coverage of campaign rallies and the political journey of NASA and Azimio in a way never witnessed in Kenya's political history.

Graphics & Design

We create animation, design and layout of images, texts and artwork on a variety of publications ranging from booklets, flyers, posters, logos & banners, billboards and many other graphic related concepts.
